# Coffee ☕

Coffee is a beloved drink that fuels mornings and sparks conversations.  
From cozy cafés to busy offices, it’s a ritual for millions worldwide.

---

## Fun Facts

- Coffee was discovered in **Ethiopia** when goats were seen dancing after eating coffee berries.
- There are over **100 species** of coffee plants, but arabica and robusta are the most common.
- The world drinks more than **2 billion cups** of coffee every day.
